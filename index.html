SDG 4.6







Goal 4.6 is: "By 2030, ensure
that all youth and a substantial
proportion of adults, both
men and women, achieve literacy
and numeracy. This page will inform
you of the problems Niger is
facing, why such skills are
important for your child,
and what you can do to

Niger currently has the worst literacy rate of any nation in the world, sitting at around 19% (2015, Our World in Data). This is largely due to lack of teachers, poor conditions of schools, and equity and financial issues with impoverished children. In comparison, the average literacy rate in the world in 2010 was 85.3%.
