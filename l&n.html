Literacy Benefits

Having a solid foundation
in the ability to read, write, speak, and
listen can lead to

- improved communication among peers,
especially with the rise of digital messaging

- utilise tools like the internet
or books to their full potential

- career advancement and success

- a better chance in going
into higher education

- an overall rise in cognitive
and technical skills

Numeracy Benefits


Having a solid foundation
in the ability to interpret and operate
with numbers can lead to

- more informed decisions financially

- career advancement and success

into higher education

- the development of logical thinking
and reasoning strategies

- the ability to perform everyday
tasks more effectively
